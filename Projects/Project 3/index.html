About Us


  About Us

  Watch it Outside is a non profit organization who loves to share the passion of cinematography with the people.

  Love Cinematography

 Boston Movie Festival is one of the biggest events in the city hosted at the Boston Public Gardens. We are glad to present 4 days of breathtaking movies. There will be something for everybody with our wide range of projections. Bostom Movie Festival is completly free of charge thanks to our partners all you need is to pre-register.

  Our master mind

 Clara Dunn is the founder and CEO of Watch it Outside events Co., with a wide experience on the cinematography industry. Her biggest desire was to share her passion with the world. Since 2012 she started to bring movies to the people, first in Brooklyn and the all across the USA.

Festivals' Fees and Rules
 There are NO entry fees all you need to do is to Pre-Register since we have a restricted capacity of 1500 People per night.
 Mobile Ramps have been provided so that all of us can enjoy Boston Movie Festival.
 Thanks to our Partners we have provided 50 additional recycle bins.
 Food and drinks Stands will be available on site, however everybody is welcome to bring his own food and drinks. The Festival is open to everybody from the youngest to the oldest people that is why No Alcohol will be sold or permited on site!
 We have a strict policy about recycling! Our moto is Keep or Bin it! Additional Bins have been provided for the purpose.
